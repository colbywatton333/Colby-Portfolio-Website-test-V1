Biography

Industrial designer focused on the intersection of material honesty and functional precision. Each project begins with a clear problem and ends with an object that justifies its own existence — nothing added without reason, nothing removed without consequence.


An independent design practice working across product, furniture, and spatial objects. Studio Colby Design operates at the boundary between craft and manufacturing — building things that are made well, last long, and carry a clear point of view.
